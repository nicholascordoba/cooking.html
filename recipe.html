Cheddar Ranch Popcorn Chicken Recipe

Upon reading this, viewers will be able to make some yummy popcorn chicken!

Ingredients!

  • Frying Oil
  • 2 fresh chicken breasts
  • 2 cups of buttermilk
  • 2 cups of cheese crackers (or any close alternative)
  • 1 packet of your favorite dressing (We will be using ranch for this example)

How to prepare & cook your chicken!

  1. First, chop your chicken breasts into small, bite-sized pieces.
  2. Secondly, in a large bowl combine and mix your chicken, buttermilk, and dressing. Then, refigerate for two hours.
  3. In a large bag, crumble your cheese crackers (or any bread crumb equivalent) into your desired size of crumb.
  4. Put your crumbs into a medium bowl and coat all of your chicken until it's fully covered.
  5. Heat oil to 350 degrees and fry your chicken until the crackers turn into a beautiful golden-brown.
  6. Place on a paper towel to dry off.
  7. Enjoy!
